template<typename TAcc, typename TParams, typename TSfinae = void>
struct alpaka::rand::engine::trait::PhiloxStatelessBaseTraits< TAcc, TParams, TSfinae >

Selection of default backend

Selects the data backend based on the accelerator device type. As of now, different backends operate on different array types.
